          PRESENTATION SCRIPT: SMARTSHELFX MODERN INVENTORY PORTAL

1. INTRODUCTION & LOGIN (THE ENTRY PORTAL)
------------------------------------------
"Welcome to the all-new SmartShelfX Inventory Management Portal. As you can see,
we've completely overhauled the UI with a premium dark glassmorphism theme. 

The Login page features a futuristic warehouse backdrop with a blurred, 
semi-transparent authentication card. Users can seamlessly log in with their
assigned roles—Admin, Manager, or Vendor. 

If you're a new user, the Registration page has been redesigned with a 
responsive two-column layout to make onboarding fast and clean."


2. ADMIN WORKFLOW (CENTRAL CONTROL)
-----------------------------------
"Once logged in as an Admin, the dashboard gives you full control. 
Key functionalities include:

- SKU MANAGEMENT: Admins can define the foundation of the inventory. In the 
  'New SKU Entry' and 'Edit SKU' screens, we've implemented the synchronized 
  dark UI. You can create SKU IDs, categorize them (like Food, Apparel, 
  Electronics), and add detailed descriptions.

- PRODUCT SALES ANALYSIS: For a top-level view, the 'Product Sales' section 
  now features a high-visibility Pie Chart. We've enlarged the chart and 
  fixed the legend overlaps to ensure every product's sales distribution is 
  crystal clear at a glance."


3. MANAGER WORKFLOW (OPERATIONAL EFFICIENCY)
--------------------------------------------
"Managers are the heart of the daily operations. Their workflow includes:

- STOCK MANAGEMENT: Adding new products into the system using the SKU 
  repository defined by the Admin.

- BUY & ISSUE TRANSACTIONS: In the 'Edit Stock' screen, Managers can buy 
  fresh stock or issue existing products. One major update here is the 
  DYNAMIC VENDOR DROPDOWN. When buying stock (Flag 1), the system now 
  fetches and displays the list of available Vendors directly from the 
  backend, ensuring data integrity and ease of entry.

- TRANSACTION MONITORING: Every stock movement is logged, allowing the 
  Manager to track inventory velocity across the warehouse."


4. VENDOR WORKFLOW (SUPPLY TRANSPARENCY)
----------------------------------------
"Vendors have a dedicated, streamlined portal. Their dashboard is designed 
for transparency:

- PRODUCT REPOSITORY: Vendors can view the current list of products and 
  monitor stock availability in real-time.

- HISTORICAL REPORTS: They have immediate access to both 'Stock IN' (Purchase) 
  and 'Stock OUT' (Issue) transaction reports, providing a clear history of 
  order fulfillment."


5. TECHNICAL UPDATES & CONCLUSION
---------------------------------
"Technically, we’ve ensured the frontend (React) and backend (Spring Boot) 
are perfectly synchronized. We resolved sensitive mapping issues in the 
Vendor list and stabilized the data-binding for all transactions.

The entire system now feels modern, intuitive, and truly enterprise-grade. 
This completes our walkthrough of the modernized SmartShelfX portal."
